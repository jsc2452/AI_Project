Monstera Deliciosa

Scientific Name: Monstera deliciosa

Description: Monstera deliciosa, also known as the Swiss cheese plant, is famous for its beautiful foliage characterized by oblong leaves with holes and splits.

Care Tips: Requires bright, indirect light. Water moderately, allowing the soil to dry out slightly between waterings.

Succulent Collection

Types: Echeveria, Sedum, Crassula, etc.

Description: Succulents are water-retaining plants adapted to arid climates. They come in various shapes, sizes, and colors, making them popular for indoor and outdoor decoration.

Care Tips: Place in bright light. Water sparingly, allowing the soil to dry out completely between waterings.

Bamboo Palm

Scientific Name: Chamaedorea seifrizii

Description: The Bamboo Palm is a popular indoor palm known for its elegant arching fronds and air-purifying qualities. It thrives in bright, indirect light.

Care Tips: Keep soil consistently moist but not waterlogged. Mist leaves occasionally to increase humidity.
